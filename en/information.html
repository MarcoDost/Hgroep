Zwolle Hogeschool

About Zwolle Hogeschool, getting the best results out of every student. That is our mission. Therefore you'll receive a lot of personal guidance during your course, and there is a lot of attention for the practical side. You'll get many options to work on your personal development, and further your boundaries.

To understand and prevent future issues.

You'll work on your ability to understand issues with a long-term effect on your future. In the education and research of De Zwolle Hogeschool the focus is on cases that are essential for the sustainability of a sector, community, geographic territory, organisation or discipline.
